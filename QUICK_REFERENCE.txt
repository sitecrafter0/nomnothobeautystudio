## WHAT YOU NEED - 3 MINUTE SUMMARY

### Create Account
1. Go to payment provider website (see links above)
2. Sign up as a merchant
3. Verify your email
4. Verify your business info

### Get Credentials  
Look for these in your dashboard and save them:

**YOCO:**
```
Public Key: pk_test_xxxxxxxxxxxxx
Secret Key: sk_test_xxxxxxxxxxxxx
```

**PAYFAST:**
```
Merchant ID: 10012345
Merchant Key: xxxxxxxxxxxxx
Passphrase: (optional but recommended)
```

**OZOW:**
```
API Key: xxxxxxxxxxxxx
Merchant ID: xxxxxxxxxxxxx
Account ID: xxxxxxxxxxxxx
```

### Send to Developer
Put credentials in a secure message with:
- Your payment provider credentials (above)
- Your website domain (e.g., https://nomnothobeauty.co.za)
- Your hosting provider name
