1 Know Your Skin Type

Before building a skincare routine, it's essential to understand your unique skin type. This knowledge is the foundation of an effective routine tailored to your needs:

Oily Skin

Characterized by excess sebum production, leading to shine and frequent breakouts. Requires lightweight, non-comedogenic products.

Dry Skin

Feels tight, flaky, or rough with visible fine lines. Needs rich moisturizers and hydrating serums to restore the skin barrier.

Combination Skin

Oily in some areas (typically the T-zone) and dry in others. Benefits from targeted, zone-specific treatments.

Sensitive Skin

Easily irritated or reactive to products. Requires gentle, fragrance-free formulations with minimal active ingredients.

Pro tip: Using products suited for your skin type makes all the difference in achieving visible results within weeks.

2 Cleanse Gently (But Effectively)

Proper cleansing is the cornerstone of any skincare routine. It removes dirt, oil, makeup residue, and environmental pollutants that accumulate throughout the day and can dull your complexion.

Essential Cleansing Tips:

  • Wash twice daily: Morning to remove overnight oil buildup and night to eliminate daily impurities
  • Avoid harsh soaps: They strip natural oils and compromise your skin barrier, leading to irritation
  • Try double cleansing: Perfect for makeup lovers ‚Äî oil cleanser first, then water-based cleanser
  • Use lukewarm water: Hot water can cause irritation; lukewarm is ideal for all skin types
