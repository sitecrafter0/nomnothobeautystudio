Shipping & Delivery

We're committed to getting your beauty essentials to you quickly and safely. Here's what you need to know:

Delivery Options

  • Standard Delivery (3-5 business days): Free on orders over R250. Cost: R50 for orders under R250.
  • Express Delivery (1-2 business days): Available for R99. Perfect for urgent orders.
  • Same-Day Delivery: Available in selected areas for R149. Order before 12 PM for same-day delivery.

Delivery Areas

  • All major cities and towns across South Africa
  • Remote areas: Additional 2-3 business days
  • International shipping: Contact us for rates

Track Your Order

How to Track

  • Check your email for the shipping confirmation with tracking number
  • Log into your account and view order history
  • Use the tracking number to monitor your package in real-time
  • Contact us if you don't receive tracking information within 24 hours

Order Status Guide

Processing: Your order is being prepared for shipment (1-24 hours)
Shipped: Your order is on its way (tracking active)
In Transit: Your package is with our courier
Out for Delivery: Your order will arrive today
Delivered: Order successfully delivered

Common Issues

  • Order delayed: Contact us with your order number and we'll investigate
  • Lost package: We'll work with the courier and replace or refund
  • Can't track order: Check your email or log into your account
