Product Description

Discover the power of our Growth Shampoo Pro, a scientifically-formulated shampoo that combines cleansing with hair growth stimulation. With biotin, keratin, and natural botanical extracts, this shampoo strengthens hair from root to tip.

Ideal for anyone experiencing hair thinning or slower growth. Regular use promotes healthier scalp, stronger hair, and visible growth results within 8-12 weeks.

Key Ingredients

Biotin

Strengthens hair and promotes growth by supporting keratin production in follicles.

Keratin

Reconstructs damaged hair and adds strength, elasticity, and shine to each strand.

Castor Oil

Rich in amino acids and nutrients, deeply nourishes scalp and promotes hair thickness.

Ginger Extract

Stimulates scalp blood circulation to improve nutrient delivery to hair follicles.

How to Use

  1. Wet Hair: Wet your hair with lukewarm water.
  2. Apply: Apply a generous amount to scalp and massage thoroughly for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Work Through: Work shampoo through hair from roots to ends.
  4. Rinse: Rinse thoroughly and follow with conditioner.

Use daily or 3-4 times per week for best results. Pair with our Premium Hair Oil for enhanced growth.
