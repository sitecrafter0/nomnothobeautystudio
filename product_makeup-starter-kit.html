Product Description

Our Makeup Starter Kit is the perfect collection for anyone starting their makeup journey or looking to expand their collection. Each item is handpicked for quality and versatility, allowing you to create endless makeup looks.

From everyday basics to bold evening looks, this 12-piece collection has you covered. The professional-grade formulations ensure smooth application and long-lasting wear. Packed in a beautiful travel case, it's perfect for both home and on-the-go touch-ups.

How to Use

  1. Prep Skin: Start with a clean, moisturized face and apply primer.
  2. Apply Foundation: Use the foundation brush for even coverage and blend outward.
  3. Eye Makeup: Apply eyeshadow using the shade palette, creating dimension with lighter and darker tones.
  4. Define Eyes: Line upper lash line with eyeliner and apply mascara to all lashes.
  5. Finish Face: Apply blush to apples of cheeks and bronzer to temples for warmth.
  6. Lips: Complete your look with your choice of lipstick shade.

All products are easy to blend and provide buildable coverage for customized makeup looks.
