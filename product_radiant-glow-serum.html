Product Description

Our Radiant Glow Serum is formulated to transform dull, tired skin into a luminous, youthful complexion. Packed with vitamin C and other potent brightening ingredients, this serum works to fade dark spots and even out skin tone.

Lightweight and silky-smooth, it absorbs instantly without leaving any sticky residue. Use morning and night for best results and experience visibly brighter, more radiant skin.

Key Ingredients

Vitamin C (L-Ascorbic Acid)

Powerful antioxidant that brightens skin and boosts collagen production for a youthful glow.

Niacinamide

Strengthens skin barrier and reduces pore appearance for smooth, radiant skin.

Hyaluronic Acid

Provides deep hydration and plumps skin for a dewy, luminous finish.

Ferulic Acid

Stabilizes vitamin C and enhances antioxidant protection for maximum brightening effects.

How to Use

  1. Cleanse: Use gentle cleanser on clean, dry skin.
  2. Apply: Dispense 2-3 drops and massage onto face and neck.
  3. Moisturize: Follow with your favorite moisturizer.
  4. Frequency: Use twice daily for optimal brightening results.

Best used in morning routine for brightening benefits and nighttime for deep rejuvenation.
