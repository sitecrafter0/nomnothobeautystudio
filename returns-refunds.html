Returns & Refunds

Return Policy

  • Returns accepted within 14 days of delivery.
  • Products must be unused, unopened, and in original packaging.
  • Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) is required.
  • Some items (e.g. opened cosmetics, gift cards) are non-returnable for hygiene reasons.

How to Request a Return

  • Contact us at admin@nomnombeautystudio.co.za or via our contact form with your order details.
  • We will provide instructions and a return shipping address.
  • Pack items securely and include your order number inside the parcel.

Return Shipping

  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer, unless the item is defective or incorrect.
  • We recommend using a trackable shipping service for your protection.

Refunds

  • Refunds are processed within 5 business days after we receive and inspect your return.
  • Refunds are issued to your original payment method.
  • You will receive an email confirmation once your refund is processed.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

  • If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos.
  • We will arrange a replacement or refund at no extra cost to you.

Returns & Refunds FAQs

We only accept returns for unused, unopened products in original packaging, unless the item is defective.
Refunds are processed within 5 business days after we receive and inspect your return. Bank processing times may vary.
Contact us within 48 hours. We will arrange a replacement or refund for incorrect items at no cost to you.
We do not offer direct exchanges. Please return your item for a refund and place a new order for the desired product.
Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

Need more help?

Contact our support team at admin@nomnombeautystudio.co.za or use our contact form.
