Shipping & Delivery

Order Processing

  • All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays).
  • You will receive an order confirmation email after purchase and a shipping notification with tracking once dispatched.

Delivery Options

  • Standard Shipping: 3-7 business days, R60 flat rate, free for orders over R800.
  • Express Shipping: 1-3 business days, R120 flat rate, available in major cities.
  • Rural/remote areas may require additional delivery time.

Delivery Coverage

  • We deliver throughout South Africa, including all major cities and most rural areas.
  • International shipping is not available at this time.

Packaging & Handling

  • All orders are carefully packed to ensure products arrive in perfect condition.
  • Eco-friendly and recyclable packaging is used wherever possible.

Tracking Your Order

  • Once shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email or SMS.
  • You can also track your order in your account dashboard if you registered at checkout.

Delays & Issues

  • Delays may occur during peak periods or due to unforeseen circumstances (e.g. weather, strikes).
  • If your order is late or missing, contact us at admin@nomnombeautystudio.co.za or via our contact form.
